Class: Calendar

Plumage.view.calendar. Calendar

new Calendar()

View that renders a selectable calendar.

Useful when incoporated in fields like DatePicker and DateRangePicker.

Not a ModelView, but keeps and updates its own view state (selectedDate, month, year,

Calendar can also limit selectable dates with minDate and maxDate, and highlight a selected range (useful in a date range picker) with fromDate and toDate.

Emitted events: prevclick, nextclick, dayclick
