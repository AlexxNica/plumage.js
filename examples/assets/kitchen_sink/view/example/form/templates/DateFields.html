These fields let you pick date or a range of dates from a calendar.

Plumage.form.fields.DateField

Field for selecting a single date

Plumage.form.fields.HourSelect

There's also an hour field, which can be combined with DateField if desired. Make sure to set 'keepTime: true' on the DateField

Plumage.form.fields.DateRangeField

Field for selecting a range of dates

You can also include hour selects in this field

Plumage.form.fields.DurationField

Field for specifying a length of time
