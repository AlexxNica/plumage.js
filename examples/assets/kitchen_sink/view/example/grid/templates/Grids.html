GridView is a wrapper around SlickGrid, a very awesome JS grid component.

A key factor in choosing SlickGrid is its support for infinite scroll. Plumage is for presenting your datasets, whatever size they are, and let's be honest, nobody wants to press 'next page' anymore.

Basic Usage

Define columns in the columns property, and pass any other SlickGrid options in with the gridOptions property.
